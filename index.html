Acquire $LBS

  1. Get a Wallet

    Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to phantom.app.

  2. Get Some SOL

    Have SOL in your wallet to swap for $LBS. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

  3. Go to Raydium or Jupiter

    Connect to Raydium or Jupiter. Go to jup.ag or raydium.io in google chrome or on the browser inside your Phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $LBS token address into Raydium or Jupiter and confirm the swap.

  4. Swap SOL for $LBS

    Swap SOL for $LBS. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
